Menu

Lasagne - An Italian Classic

Layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich fillings—typically a hearty meat ragù (sauce), creamy besciamella (bechamel) sauce, and a blend of cheeses like mozzarella, ricotta, and Parmesan. Baked al forno (in the oven) until piping hot and golden. A comforting, flavorful favorite.

Burger - The Flavorful Flame-Broiled Burger

A satisfying beef burger, often flame-broiled for a smoky, distinct flavor. Traditionally served on a toasted bun with classic toppings and a special sauce, possibly drawing inspiration from the original American fast-food chain or its popular Norwegian/Swedish namesake, which translates to "Beef Burger."

Kebab Pizza - A Swedish-Turkish Fusion

A unique pizza creation popular in Sweden, combining Italian and Turkish flavors. Featuring a pizza base topped with tomato sauce and cheese, it's finished with thin slices of seasoned kebab meat (often beef or chicken), fresh vegetables like lettuce and onions, and a generous drizzle of a creamy, sometimes spicy, garlic or yogurt-based kebab sauce.
